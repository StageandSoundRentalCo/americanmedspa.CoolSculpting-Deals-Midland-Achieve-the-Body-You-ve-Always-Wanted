What is CoolSculpting®?

CoolSculpting® is a non-surgical procedure that targets and eliminates stubborn fat cells using cooling technology. This procedure works by freezing fat cells, which are then naturally eliminated by your body over time. It’s perfect for people close to their goal weight but with areas of fat that won’t go away through diet and exercise alone.

CoolSculpting® targets areas like:

  • Abdomen
  • Thighs
  • Flanks (love handles)
  • Chin
  • Upper arms
  • Back
  • Bra fat

One of the best things about CoolSculpting® is that it’s non-invasive. There are no needles, no incisions, and no downtime, making it an excellent option for those who want to contour their bodies without the hassle of surgery.

How CoolSculpting® Works: The Process Explained

The CoolSculpting® treatment is relatively simple and comfortable. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown of what you can expect:

  1. Consultation: Your journey begins with a free consultation where we assess your goals, discuss your treatment options, and determine the best areas for CoolSculpting®.
  2. Treatment: On the day of your treatment, you’ll be comfortably reclined while a gel pad and applicator are placed on the targeted area. The applicator delivers controlled cooling to the fat cells beneath your skin.
  3. Relax During Treatment: The cooling sensation may initially feel slightly uncomfortable, but it quickly becomes numb. You can relax, read, or even nap during the procedure, typically 35-60 minutes per area.
  4. Post-Treatment Care: There’s no downtime after the procedure, and you can resume normal activities immediately. You may experience some mild redness, swelling, or tingling, but these effects usually resolve within a few hours to days.
  5. Results: Fat cells begin to break down and are naturally eliminated from your body over 1-3 months. You’ll notice the full results after this period, with more defined contours and a slimmer appearance.
